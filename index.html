About This Album

"Portals is the third studio album by American singer-songwriter Melanie Martinez. It was released on March 31, 2023, through Atlantic Records. It was preceded by two singles, "Death", on March 17, 2023, and "Void" on March 29, 2023. A deluxe edition was released on April 5, 2023, featuring three new tracks." -Wikipedia
